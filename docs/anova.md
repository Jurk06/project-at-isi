# One-Way ANOVA Analysis

## Overview

This section presents the **ANOVA (Analysis of Variance)** analysis performed on the water well dataset. ANOVA helps identify whether there are statistically significant differences between well characteristics across different Indian states.

## Methodology

- **Type**: One-way ANOVA (single independent variable - state)
- **Purpose**: Test for significant differences between groups
- **Dependent Variables**: Well depth, water availability metrics

## Key Results

The ANOVA analysis revealed:
- **Significant regional differences** in well depth across states
- **P-values** indicating statistical significance of variations
- **State-wise comparison** showing which regions face more severe water challenges

## Interpretation

States with higher F-statistics and lower p-values show more significant variation in well characteristics, indicating diverse hydrological and geographical conditions.
